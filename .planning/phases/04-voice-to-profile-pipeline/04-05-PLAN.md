<threat_model>
## Security Threat Model

**Threats and mitigations:**
- **Error envelope leaks:** 502 must not include raw model output, Python tracebacks, or `detail` with stack; only `message` and `status` in global exception handler.
- **Duplicate error strings:** one constant in `qwen_service` or `routers` for the English message, reused by WS and HTTP.
</threat_model>
